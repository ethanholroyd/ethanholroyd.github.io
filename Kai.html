Kai: Lost Waters

Download Windows and Mac Builds

In this project, I collaborated with 4 other students to create a dynamic 2D puzzle platform using water physics. My tasks included player movement, hitboxes and health, camera movement, checkpoint systems, and enemy design and AI. I also contributed to implementing the art and animation, level design, and other mechanics. In this project, we used Jira to track our tasks and Unity to develop the game. We tied for first place in the game design showcase event that followed the project.
